    Bean Burgers

    This is very easy to make and only has a few readily available ingredients.

    Preparation time
    about 10 mins
    Cooking time
    30 minutes in a conventional Oven or 10 minutes frying
    makes 4 pies
    freeze before cooking

    Ingredients

    • 4 cans of beans of your choice I use Black Eyed Beans, red Kidney Beans, Brorlotti beans and Butter Beans
    • 1 red Onion
    • 1 Flax Egg ( 2 desert spoons of Flaxseed 6 desert spoons of Hot Water
    • seasoning ( to taste)
      • <li 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil </li?
      • 3 teaspoon garlic powder
      • 3 teaspoons of cumin
      • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper

    Method

    Step 1) Make the Flax Egg, this is simply either flaxseed or chai seed and water, I use 1 dessertspoon of flaxseed and 3 dessertspoons of water. Add boiling water to the flaxseed and mix well. then leave to cool..

    Step 2) Chop the Onion and then pulse in a food mixer. I am not very keen on Onions so I make this really fine but you may like it chunky !

    Step 3) Add the beans and pulse or mix until you have either a fine or coarse bean mixture. My food processor seemed to produce a mixture of both consistencies

    Step 5) Seasoning - add the seasoning to the mixture and stir well, feel free to add different amounts and types. the stiring didnt work well in the food mixer so I put the ingredients in a bowl and sired by hand.

    Step 6) Make the burgers by hand, to get the same about per burger I used 2 large dessertspoons of mixture per burger and then formed by hand.

    Step 7) cook for around 30 mins or so in the oven or fry for 5 minutes each side with a little vegetable oil.

    Item Value per burger
    Calories 139
    Protein 9g
    Carbohydrates 17g
    Fiber 8g
    Fat 2g of which 0 is saturated

    These burgers are packed with beans, the clue is in the name ! Batch make them they are a good thing to pull out of the freezer as and when needed.

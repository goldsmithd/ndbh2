    Lentil and Bean Pies

    This is easy to make and only has a few readily available ingredients. There are two options but the one with the potato top isn’t really a pie and more resembles a cottage pie, still delicious though!

    Preperation time
    about 10 mins
    Cooking time
    20 minutes in a conventional Oven

    Ingredients

    • 120g green lentils
    • 1 can cannellini beans or your beans of choice
    • 1 can Borlotti beans or your beans of choice
    • 125g of garden peas
    • 175g carrots
    • Shortcrust pastry enough for 6 lids ( 1 pack of easy roll 375g) or 600g white potatoes for the toppings
    • 200mg Almond milk if using potatoes for topping
    • 1 vegetarian stock cube
    • sesoning
      • 1 ½ teaspoons gravy granules
      • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
      • 1 ½ teaspoons of cumin
      • 1 ½ teaspoons of cayenne pepper

    Method

    ##image is 427x240

    Step 1) Cook lentils – cover with water and bring to the boil then let simmer for around 20 mins. If you are using potatoes as lids then boil these for around the same time.

    Step 2) Make the gravy - crumble the veg stock cube and add the gravey granules into around 1 pint, or more if you like. Microwave for 2 mins and stir then microwave for another minute when you are going to add to the lentils.

    Step 3) Add beans - once the lentils are cooked add the beans, I aways drain first because they come with preservative water in most tins which you dont need. If you are using potatoes then mash these with the Almond milk or you can use margerine if you wish.

    Step 4) Add carrots and peas - Chop carrots finely, no need to peel them just a quick scrub and top and tail them add to lentils, add the garden peas, these can be straight from frozen.

    Step 5) Seasoning - add the seasoning to the mixture and stir well, feel free to add different amounts and types.

    Step 6) decant into pie containers or use cookwear or thing that will be an appropriate oven proof container. Add the lids and decorate with whatever design you wish, or add the potato at this point.

    Step 7) cook for around 30 mins or so on around 180degrees for a fan ovenf or 200degrees for a conventional oven. Any left over pies can be frozen prior to cooking. Serve with loads of Veg and the left over gravy.
